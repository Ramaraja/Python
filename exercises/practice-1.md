### Part 1 — Print & Input

6. Convert kilometers to meters  

### Part 3 — Conditions

8. Check if someone is eligible to vote (age ≥ 18)

### Part 4 — Loops
10. Print numbers from 1 to 10

### Part 5 — Strings
